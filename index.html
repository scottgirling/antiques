About

Based in Sheffield, South Yorkshire, Yasott Antiques buys, repairs and refurbishes antiques from across the globe.

We started thrifting art as a side hustle to help fund our university studies, finding hidden gems in some of Bristol's best second hand stores. What was once a part time gig for a little extra beer money has become a full time operation.

We've enjoyed flea markets since time began but it was whilst backpacking through Asia we realised how severe our addiction antiques and bargain hunting really was!!

We are now the UK's largest South East Asian antique dealers; stocking mostly art, books and currency.

We hope you like our collection!
