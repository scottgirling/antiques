About

Based in Sheffield, South Yorkshire, Yasott Antiques buys, repairs and refurbishes antique artwork, books and currency.

What started out as a side hustle to help fund our university studies has now become a full time operation.

We've always enjoyed flea markets but it was whilst backpacking through Asia we found where our interests truly lie.

We are now the UK's largest South East Asian antique dealers.

We hope you like our collection!
